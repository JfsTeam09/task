JUNGLE BOOK

The Jungle Book is a 2016 American adventure drama film directed and produced by Jon Favreau, written by Justin Marks and produced by Walt Disney Pictures. Based on Rudyard Kipling's eponymous collective works, this film is a live-action/CGI remake of Walt Disney's 1967 animated feature film of the same title
